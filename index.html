WHY EXOD INTERNATIONAL

EXOD International helps Korean beauty brands
build stable, high-visibility market presence across Canada
through retail access, compliance, and nationwide logistics.

MULTICHANNEL

Online & Offline Distribution Channels

PERFORMANCE

Performance-Driven Marketing Execution

LOGISTICS NETWORK

Our Toronto headquarters and Vancouver warehouse create a strong bicoastal supply chain, helping us manage inventory efficiently and deliver quickly across every region in Canada.
